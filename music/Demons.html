Demons



2012 Imagine Dragons

We all have some sort of demons inside us. We all fight them. We don’t always succeed in defeating them, but we try just the same. In this song, in which the band sounds like an Americanized Coldplay, the protagonist is waging that internal battle.

This song means that we all have secrets and we all try to solve our problems alone. But when you see someone's eyes, you find pain in them of their secrets. We create our own world by our secrets which is very different from the real one. The main theme of this song is adressing the dark side inside of you, the one which you are not proud of, which convinces you that you are a failure by your past mistakes and acknowledging them by bringing them to light and not getting pulled down by them..

1. Things are not always what they seem

Things are not always what they seem, except, perhaps, when things start going south? Is that when the true colors are seen and when someone shows they are made of gold? Or do they let their dark side prevail? You find out if the tough get going when the going gets tough. When days are rough and you have to fold cards - give up, like in poker. And wherever you seek help, you are let down. People reveal their true shades. .

2. You cannot bury or drown the dark side.

Despite his best efforts, the narrator cannot bury or drown the dark side. He is making a valiant effort to protect someone from experiencing that element of his personality, but it doesn’t seem to be working. Perhaps it’s best to stop stuffing it down and let it out and move on, since trying and failing to suffocate it can only exacerbate the problem and create further tension. Resolve the conflict and move on, maybe?

3. Look in the brighter side of everything

song talks about protecting someone innocent from the inherent evil inside him. But it follows up by saying that there's nowhere to hide since everyone has some inherent evil in them. It says that no matter what they create, the evil will still be with them at the end of the day. If you look at ones eyes will make it perfectly clear to the you.So it is better to look brighter side of everything rather than dark side .

4. Similarity between stage and real life.

A curtain call is the return of the performer to the stage at the end of the performance to take a last bow, once applauded by the audience. So he draws an analogy between the stage and the life here. He says that at the end of his life, he has to face the audience for the last time. And once the lights all go off and the show is over, the ones who haven't played a justifiable role in their lives, are further incapable of walking.

4. Experience the end of your life.

The people have dug his grave since his end his nigh. A masquerade is a form of a celebration which involves dancing with masks on the face of the participants so that they can't be identified. He again brilliantly draws an analogy between people calling out at the mess he's made, from the masquerade - referring to the people who criticise him anonymously for every wrong act he did on the stage.
He cherishes the innocence that 'you' possesses. Perhaps not all of us have inherent evils. So he wants to save 'you' from all the lifetime of misery he had to go through and he cannot find another way, unless 'you' shows him how.
